About me

Building things
that matter.

I'm a product builder who loves the full stack of getting an idea to market — from scribbling on a whiteboard to shipping something real that people actually use. I believe great products live at the intersection of sharp thinking, honest craft, and genuine user empathy.

Over the years I've worked across early-stage startups and growth-stage companies, usually found somewhere between engineering, design, and strategy. I enjoy the messy, exciting work of figuring out what to build before optimizing how to build it.

Outside of work, I think a lot about systems, interfaces, and why certain things feel good to use and others don't. I'm always tinkering on side projects — some ship, some don't, all of them teach me something.
